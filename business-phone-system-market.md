<p><p>The business phone system market consists of two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed and managed locally within a company’s infrastructure, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted off-site by service providers, allowing businesses to access features via the internet, reducing hardware costs, and enabling easier scalability. Cloud solutions typically offer flexibility, lower initial costs, and simplified management for modern businesses.</p></p>
