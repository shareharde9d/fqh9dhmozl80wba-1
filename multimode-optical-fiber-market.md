<p><strong>The Multimode Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>50/125μm</li><li>62.5/125μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multimode optical fiber market primarily consists of two key types: 50/125μm and 62.5/125μm fibers. The 50/125μm fiber has a smaller core diameter, allowing for higher bandwidth and transmission speeds, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, the 62.5/125μm fiber features a larger core, which facilitates easier coupling with light sources but generally offers lower bandwidth. Both types are widely used in data centers, local area networks, and telecommunications owing to their advantages in short-distance transmission.</p></p>
