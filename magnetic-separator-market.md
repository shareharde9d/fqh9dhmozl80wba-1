<p><p>The magnetic separator market comprises various types, including tubular, square, and roller magnetic separators. Tubular magnetic separators are designed for liquid applications, efficiently removing contaminants in pipelines. Square magnetic separators, often used in bulk material handling, are effective for trapping ferrous particles from powders and granules. Roller magnetic separators utilize a rotating magnetic roller to separate fine ferrous particles from dry material, ensuring high efficiency and purity in products. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs for material separation and contamination control.</p></p>
